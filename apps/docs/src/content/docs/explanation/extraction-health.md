The **Extraction Health** screen is a live map of the work: where the data rests, and every place it
moves from one step to the next.

## Drawers and seams

- **Drawers** are where the data rests between steps (for example, a stored paper, or a finished
  result waiting for review).
- **Seams** are the places the data moves from one step to the next.

The system watches at the **seams**, because that is where one step hands off to the next, and where a
problem would show up first.

## Real activity only

Everything on this screen is real activity from actual runs, with exact counts. Nothing is sampled,
estimated, or filled in for show. If a measure is not being recorded yet, it is left out rather than
faked.

## Two ways to look

- **Precise view** — exact counts and timings at each seam, for when you want the numbers.
- **Flow view** — the same activity shown as data moving through the system, for the overall shape.

Both are built from the same real activity; they are two ways of reading it.
